Hi. I'm Alicia Zaludova.

But you can call me Alice. It's appropriate for the wonderland I've entered since beginning to learn web development. A couple things happened that led me to explore this field of endless possibilities.

A year ago, a friend said to me "You should learn how to code." The way i remember it, the suggestion came out of nowhere, but it must have been based on his familiarity with (i.e. tolerance of) a couple personality traits: an irrepressible attentiveness to detail, critically-thinking just about everything, and curiosity run amok. These are things I cannot help.

After a lot of research into various programs I found out about the Nashville Software School, which stood out among the programs I'd been researching. It is a non-profit with close ties to the tech community in Nashville. I decided to learn full stack development in Evening Cohort 7.
